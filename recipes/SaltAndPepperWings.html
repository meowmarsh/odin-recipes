Salt and Pepper Wings

Description

The salt and pepper wings are a family favourite! They're easy to make and can be air fried for later.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken wings
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 shallots
  • 2 stalks green onion
  • white pepper

Steps

  1. Wash & cut the wings
  2. Marinate wings
  3. Prepare ingredients
  4. Create seasoning and coat chicken
